Initialize Sys\+Tick timer for 1 ms interrupts. 

Configures Sys\+Tick to generate interrupts every 1 ms at 100 MHz system clock. Enables Sys\+Tick interrupt and starts the timer. Must be called during system initialization before using \doxylink{systick_8h_acd8d13c328bebdf9eddea27ff9448c2d}{millis()} or \doxylink{systick_8h_ab7cce8122024d7ba47bf10f434956de4}{delay\+\_\+ms()}.
