\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Per-\/motor throttle bias adjustments. 

Allows individual motor trim to compensate for manufacturing variations, weight distribution, or damaged propellers. Values are normalized (0.\+0-\/1.\+0) and added to the base throttle + \doxylink{structPID}{PID} corrections.
